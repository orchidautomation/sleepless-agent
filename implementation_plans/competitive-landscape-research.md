### Founder Story

**Flo Crivello** raised $50M for Teamflow (remote work/virtual office tool during pandemic). When he realized it had no future, he executed a brutal pivot:

- Shut down Teamflow entirely
- Fired entire go-to-market team to save runway
- Built 70,000-person waitlist using Twitter ("brain farts" and building in public)
- Rebuilt product from scratch ("Lindy 2.0")

### Positioning Evolution (Critical Lesson)

| Positioning | Result |
|-------------|--------|
| "AI Employee" | **Failed** - too confusing, people didn't understand |
| "Zapier for AI" | **Worked** - clear mental model, instant understanding |

**Key insight:** People need a reference point. "Zapier for AI" immediately communicates value.

## Slack-Native Players

### Slack's Agentforce (Salesforce)

Salesforce's massive bet on AI agents in Slack:

- **Channel Expert:** Out-of-box agent that answers FAQs in any channel
- **@mention agents:** Interact with AI agents like human teammates
- **Slack Agent Templates:** Pre-built solutions for onboarding, support, insights
- **Native CRM integration:** Full Salesforce data access

**Scale:** Fortune 50 companies, enterprise-grade

### People.ai Bot for Slack

- Captures Slack conversations → syncs to CRM
- Solves "missing CRM data" problem
- Pairs with SalesAI product for coaching
- Target: Enterprise sales teams

### ClearFeed

- Customer support in Slack
- 4.9/5 G2 rating
- $59/seat/month starting
- B2B focus

### Pylon

- B2B customer support via Slack
- Shared Slack channels with customers
- Ticket management in Slack
- $59/seat/month

## Market Validation

### YC Investment Thesis

Y Combinator is actively funding AI assistant startups:
- 141 AI Assistant startups in their portfolio
- Multiple recent batches (W26, S25, F25) include this category
- Focus areas: Sales, Email, Calendar, Productivity

### Funding Trends

| Company | Amount | Round | Date |
|---------|--------|-------|------|
| Lindy | $35M | Series B | 2023 |
| Fyxer | $30M | Series B | Sept 2025 |
| Various YC | $500K | Seed | 2024-2025 |

**Signal:** Significant VC interest in AI assistants that take actions

### Common Patterns

1. **Slack is a key interface** - Multiple companies building Slack-first
2. **Sales/SDR is hot vertical** - Caretta, People.ai, Lindy all target sales
3. **Integration count matters** - Lindy touts 1,600+, used as credibility signal
4. **Case studies sell** - Concrete ROI numbers ("30 hours saved") drive conversions
5. **Positioning clarity critical** - "Zapier for AI" > "AI Employee"

## Lessons for Our GTM

### From Lindy

1. **Build in public** - Flo built 70K waitlist via Twitter
2. **Positioning > Product** - "Zapier for AI" unlocked growth
3. **Case studies with numbers** - "30 hours saved" is concrete
4. **Integration count is marketing** - Tout the number prominently

### From Caretta

1. **Slack-native is differentiator** - "Lives in Slack" is the pitch
2. **Sales is hot vertical** - YC keeps funding sales AI
3. **Knowledge compounds** - "Gets smarter with every call"

### From April

1. **Interface innovation matters** - Voice-first is their moat
2. **Target unserved market** - "People who need EAs but can't afford them"
3. **Mobile-first for certain use cases** - Manage inbox while commuting
